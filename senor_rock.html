The Señor Rock Bar And Grill

By PAOLA GARCIA | November 02, 2021



The Señor Rock Bar and Grill is a combination restaurant, pop/rock museum, and sports bar located in Puerto Plata's Playa Dorada Mall. It's just a short walking distance from both Playa Dorada Beach and the resort complex.


When you first walk into Señor Rock you can't help but notice its cool decor. First, there's their trademark purple, life-sized replica, 1957 Chevy Bel Air Convertible that hangs high above their spacious rectangular bar. Then there are the restaurant's walls which are adorned with pop and rock and roll memorabilia from some of the greatest artists of all time. The items of Rihanna, Elvis, Bob Marley, John Lennon, Jimi Hendrix, and many, many others decorate the walls. Señor Rock is literally a miniature rock and roll museum and hall of fame.


Now just because you're away from home on a trip, that doesn't mean you have to miss that big game, fight or sporting event. Sports fans, Señor Rock has you covered! Watch live NBA, NFL, NHL, NCAA, MLB, and the UFC all on their big screen.


If you are looking for a change of pace from your resort's food or if you just want to try out a nice, casual restaurant with delicious food, visit Señor Rock. They offer a nice variety of reasonably priced appetizers, hamburgers, chicken and pasta dishes, steaks, seafood and my personal favorite, the barbequed ribs.


Señor Rock also offers a wide selection of alcoholic beverages. Try out the local favorite, Presidente Beer or choose from the numerous selection of imported beers they carry. Try out their house favorite the CoronaRita or any of their other outstanding cocktails and margaritas.


So the next time you're in Puerto Plata, pay The Señor Rock Bar and Grill a visit. They are conveniently located in the Playa Dorada Mall right next to the resorts and the beach. It's decor and music memorabilia make it a great place to visit and take pictures. The numerous sports packages it carries make it a great place to go watch sporting events. Plus they serve a variety of reasonably priced, tasty food and drinks that hit the spot.
