Dominican Cake Bosses

By PAOLA GARCIA | March 01, 2022


Like most people, Dominicans enjoy celebrating special occasions with family and friends. We especially love celebrating them with cakes. Now we're not just talking about a regular cake. We are talking about some of the most beautiful, artistically creative, themed cakes you will ever lay eyes on! Whether it's an anniversary, birthday, baby shower, graduation, wedding, etc., it's virtually mandatory to get one of these cakes if you are a Dominican.


The makers of these masterpieces, the Dominican Cake Bosses, place their heart, soul, and immense baking skills into creating these culinary works of art that taste every bit as good as they look. Driven by a competitive creative passion and a desire to meet their customer's high expectations, these bosses are constantly outdoing themselves with one elaborate and delicious cake right after the other. For them, it's more than just baking cakes. It's a labor of love.


It's my pleasure to share with you some of the eye-catching work of a couple of these bosses. Special thanks to the cake bosses at Puerto Plata's very own Caliche Reposteria Bakery and Carmen's Dominican Cakes of Amityville, New York.
