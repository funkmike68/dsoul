THE PAPAYA AND BANANA
TROPICAL SMOOTHIE

By GLENNY GOMEZ | February 08, 2022


The Papaya & Banana Tropical Smoothie is very easy to make, smooth tasting, refreshing and very healthy. This wonderful smoothie is rich in vitamins C and B6, potassium, antioxidants and is low in fat.


The Papaya & Banana Tropical Smoothie makes up to 4, 12 ounce servings. It makes a great light meal or a snack. *For the best taste results use fresh fruit, especially the papaya.


Ingredients

  • two cups fresh ripe papaya
  • two cups of evaporated milk
  • two ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup of sugar or honey
  • one cup of vanilla yogurt
  • one teaspoon of cinnamon



Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a blender.

  2. Blend at medium speed for approximately 90 seconds for a smoother and creamier smoothie.

  3. Serve immediately and Enjoy!
