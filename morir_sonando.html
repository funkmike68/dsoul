MORIR SOÑANDO

To Die Dreaming"

By PAOLA GARCIA | November 15, 2021


MORIR SOÑANDO, which means, "to die dreaming" is arguably the most popular non-alcoholic beverage in the entire Dominican Republic.


MORIR SOÑANDO is a winning combination of citrus and low-fat dairy. Very nutritious, easy to make, delicious, and so refreshing. A real thirst quencher on any day! Serves up to six people.


Ingredients

  • 4 cups of ice cold evaporated milk
  • 2 1/2 cups of chilled orange juice
  • 2 cups of ice
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla flavoring



Instructions

  1. Combine the milk, orange juice, sugar and vanilla flavoring in a blender and blend until smooth.

  2. Place your ice in a pitcher. Slowly pour the blended mixture over the ice and stir.

  3. Serve immediately.
